====== 13. Tepejanjiong NuꞌAlataꞌala Concoono Tetoo-too NuꞌIsrael ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-01.jpg}}

Lasiambulang touk nesuvung lamai teMesir, teꞌAlataꞌala nomimpin siira nalabat tetana sanu noodoy umaꞌo eibuut sanu tinope teBuut nuSinai. Eini tebuut sanu selio-lio eipaio siMusa neꞌita terui sanu naꞌarampunong. Tetoo-too nongoroa tetenda-tenda eipuu nubuut heꞌua.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-02.jpg}}

TeꞌAlataꞌala nonjarita eisiMusa ane eitoo-too nuꞌIsrael, “Ane siꞌemiu monuruk Siaꞌu ane tepejanjiongU, siꞌemiu kana majari tebangsa sanu Upilei, tepomarenta nuꞌimam-imam, ane tebangsa sanu mapacing.” 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-03.jpg}}

Tolu eleo lamai heꞌua, touk tetoo-too mesiapo teambok niira, teꞌAlataꞌala nenyau eiBuut nuSinai concoono tegorung, teꞌilat, teobol ane tesuara nutorompet sanu noporos. Boi siMusa sanu vinee nolalaꞌe eibuut heꞌua. 



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-04.jpg}}

Lamai heꞌua teꞌAlataꞌala nogutu tepejanjiong concoono siira ane nonjarita, “Siaꞌumo teSiopu, teꞌAlataꞌala niꞌoo, sanu nonyalamata siꞌemiu lamai tepevotuanong eiMesir. Inyaa monyomba tedewa ntaninya.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-05.jpg}}

“Inyaa mogutu tebarhala antau monyombanya, karna Siaꞌu, teSiopu, teꞌAlataꞌala sanu meꞌirihati. Inyaa masala monope tetopeꞌU. Molambot ane monjagai teꞌapacing eieleo Pengondok. Gauka jojoo tepokarajaaong niꞌoo eilalong ono eleo, karna eieleo pepitu heꞌua teeleo siꞌemiu mengondok untuk molambot Siaꞌu.”


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-06.jpg}}

“Noongkoi siamamu ane siinamu. Inyaa momate. Inyaa monyaapi. Inyaa monganggaa. Inyaa mongakal. Inyaa meꞌeseilua telapi nutagumu, tevonuanya, atau tesapa umai eisiia.”


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-07.jpg}}

Lamai heꞌua teꞌAlataꞌala nonulis Sompulu teParenta-Nya, eiromboꞌa tevatu ane nombeenaꞌonya eisiMusa. 





{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-10.jpg}}

Tetoo-too nuꞌIsrael nonarima tepejanjiong nuꞌAlataꞌala ane seilu monuruk tehukum sanu vineena nuAlataꞌala eisiira, boi monyomba teꞌAlataꞌala, ane majari tetoo-too sanu pinilei-Nya. Tapi jio neende touk siira nojanji untuk nomarcaya eiꞌAlataꞌala, siira odosa majaat tututuu.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-11.jpg}}

Apampulueleo, siMusa umai eiBuut nuSinai netepojaok concoono teꞌAlataꞌala. Tetoo-too nuꞌIsrael mabasa metaang siMusa metencile. Karna heꞌua siira nongomung tebulaan eisiHarun ane nomongi mogutua teberhala untuk siira! 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-12.jpg}}

SiHarun nogutu teberhala lamai tebulaan sima teunga nusapi. Tetoo-too nuꞌIsrael nompamulamo nonyomba ane nombee tepombee eibarhala heꞌua! TeꞌAlataꞌala nongoungkul tututuu karna tedosa niira ane morancanaa momate siira. Tapi siMusa nonggane untuk siira, ane teꞌAlataꞌala nomoꞌinepea teganenya ane jio nomate siira. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-13.jpg}}

Watu siMusa nenyau lamai tebuut ane neꞌita tebarhala heꞌua, siia nongoungkul tututuu ane siia nopoꞌorua romboꞌa tevatu eipaio teꞌAlataꞌala nonulis Sompulu teParenta-Nya. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-14.jpg}}

Then Moses beat the idol into powder, threw the powder into some water and made the people drink the water. God sent a plague on the people and many of them died.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-15.jpg}}

Lamai heꞌua siia nopoꞌorua teberhala heꞌua jaok najari teavu, norampaka teavu heꞌua eilalong nuogo ane nomaksa tetoo-too nuꞌIsrael nenginung teogo heꞌua. TeꞌAlataꞌala nonjaoka tepees eitoo-too heꞌua ane nabari sanu naate. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-08.jpg}}

TeꞌAlataꞌala nombee valii mabari tehukum ane teꞌaturan ntaninya sanu kanaboi noturuꞌi. Ane tetoo-too heꞌua monuruꞌi tehukum-hukum eini, teꞌAlataꞌala nojanji Siia kana mombarakai ane monjagai siira. Ane siira jio monuruꞌi tehukum-hukum heꞌua, teꞌAlataꞌala kana mohukum siira. TeꞌAlataꞌala nonuju tetoo-too nuꞌIsraꞌel mogutu sobua tetenda sanu kusus. Heꞌua notope teTenda nuPejajaoꞌong. TeTenda nuPejajaoꞌong umai rolontang, inontoi tekaing ntinoena sanu mooge. Boi teꞌImam sanu Mooge sanu maala mentama eisabata nukaing heꞌua sontoung sangkaning, karna eilalong nulontang sanu mapacing heꞌua inembea tepejanjiong nuꞌAlataꞌala eisobua tepeti sanu mapacing tinope tepomoianong nuꞌAlataꞌala. 



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-09.jpg}}

Tetoo sanu jio monuruk tehukum nuꞌAlataꞌala kanaboi mongomung tebinaatang eiporampunong tepombee sanu eitolo nuTenda nuPejajaoꞌong untuk mombee tepombee eiꞌAlataꞌala. Sotoo teꞌimam otugas monyambale sambaang tebinaatang noꞌomung ane norampuni eimeja heꞌua. Teraa nupombee heꞌua tepombayar tehukuman nudosa tetoo sanu mombeea tepombee heꞌua ane mogutu siia melivas eimata nuꞌAlataꞌala. TeꞌAlataꞌala nomile tesiaꞌa niMusa, siHarun, ane tebijanya majari teꞌimam-imam-Nya. 

//A Bible story from: Exodus 19-34//
