==== Mondulu! ====

Siꞌami seilu mogutu tebuku eini eilalong //jojoo tebasa eijuniaa// ane siꞌoo maala mondulu! Teꞌeseseilu eini maala majari.  Siꞌami nomikir, ane jojoo tetoo-too nuKristen mokaraja concoono ane mombatua ane mombee tebuku eini. 

==== Mombee Mabebas ====

Mombee tebuku eini eitoo-too ntaninya. Jojoo teversi sanu digital maala noꞌala online.  Karna siꞌami nepake teisin sanu mabebas, siꞌoo maala mobaluk tebuku eini eipapaio eijuniaa ane jio porlu mombayar tesapa-sapa eisiꞌami! Mombasa ntaninya sima eini ei  http://openbiblestories.com.

==== Mongulara! ====

Mongala Open Bible Stories sima tevideo-video ane sanu maala noꞌembe eihp eilalong tebasa-basa ntaninya ei http://openbiblestories.com. Ei tesitus web, siꞌoo maala notuluni mombatua Open Bible Stories eilalong tebasa //niꞌoo//.
